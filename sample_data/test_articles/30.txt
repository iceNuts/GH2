A seminar on a future vision for the Egyptian media was held on Saturday 30/3/2013 at the Media Production City in the 6th of October City. The gathering was attended by representatives of media outlets, some political parties and movements and media experts.

Information Minister Salah Abdel Maksoud said that the seminar was a consultative meeting that was meant to reach an agreement on a future vision for the Egyptian media.

Abdel Maksoud asserted the keenness of the political leadership and the government on preserving media freedom.
